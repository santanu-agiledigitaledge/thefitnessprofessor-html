Meet The Fitness Professor

DOUG DORSEY - Expert guidance for lasting results

Doug Dorsey, also known as "The Fitness Professor," is a best-selling author, personal trainer, and transformation coach with over 20 years of experience in the wellness industry and 25 years in education. He began his career helping women lose weight, gain strength, and feel better in their bodies.

Today, Doug helps women over 40 build confidence, increase energy, and create a lifestyle they can feel proud of. His goal is simple: to help you feel strong, capable, and fully present in the life you want to live.

Over time, Doug saw that weight loss alone wasn’t the full story. The real challenge was helping people keep it off and create healthy habits that last. That insight led him to develop a proven system for sustainable fat loss, designed especially for women navigating fitness in midlife.
