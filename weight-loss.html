Beyond Weight Loss: Full-Body Support for Total Wellness

Your transformation isn’t just about the scale — we support your energy, recovery, and habits with real, science-backed tools.

T-Shape 2 Body Contouring

T-Shape2 smooths cellulite, reduces stubborn fat, and firms your skin—without surgery, needles, or recovery time. Designed for comfort, each treatment helps you feel more confident in every area you choose to treat.

TruFlex + TruSculpt

Our non-invasive body contouring treatments tone muscle and melt fat fast. truFlex® delivers the power of 54,000 crunches in one session to strengthen and define, while truSculpt® targets stubborn fat, reducing up to 24% in key areas.

Red Light Therapy

Red Light Therapy uses low-level light to reduce inflammation, speed up recovery, improve skin tone, and support fat metabolism. It’s completely non-invasive, pain-free, and perfect for boosting results before or after workouts.

IV Therapy

Infuse your body with essential vitamins, minerals, and hydration—delivered directly into your bloodstream for fast, effective results. Whether you’re feeling run-down, recovering from illness, or optimizing for fat loss, there’s a custom drip for you.

Supplements

We partner with Thorne® to offer professional-grade supplements that support metabolism, hormone balance, energy, and overall wellness. Every formula is third-party tested, physician-recommended, and tailored to your body’s unique needs.

Nutrition + Exercise
