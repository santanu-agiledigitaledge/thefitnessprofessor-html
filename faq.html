Programs & Services

Questions about our fitness programs and coaching services

What is The Perfect Scale program?

The Perfect Scale is our signature 16-week weight loss program designed specifically for women over 40. It combines personalized nutrition, strength training, and holistic wellness strategies to help you lose weight, build strength, and create sustainable healthy habits.

How does the 16-week program work?

The program is structured in phases, starting with assessment and goal setting, followed by personalized meal planning, exercise routines, and weekly coaching sessions. You'll receive step-by-step guidance, progress tracking, and ongoing support throughout your transformation journey.

Do I need a gym membership to participate?

No! Our programs are designed to work at home with minimal equipment. We provide bodyweight exercises and routines that can be done in your living room. However, if you have access to a gym, we can incorporate that into your personalized plan.

What's the difference between group training and personal training?

Group training offers a supportive community environment with scheduled sessions and group accountability. Personal training provides one-on-one attention with customized programs tailored specifically to your needs, goals, and schedule. Both options include nutrition guidance and ongoing support.

Can I join if I'm a beginner?

Absolutely! Our programs are designed for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to those with more experience. We'll assess your current fitness level and create a program that's appropriate for where you are right now, with modifications and progressions built in.

What is the Align program?

Align is our comprehensive wellness program that focuses on balancing hormones, improving metabolism, and creating alignment between your body, mind, and lifestyle. It addresses stress management, sleep optimization, and metabolic health for women in midlife.

Nutrition & Meal Planning

Questions about our nutrition programs and meal plans

Will I receive a personalized meal plan?

Yes! Every program includes a customized nutrition plan based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, and goals. We'll work with foods you enjoy and create a plan that fits your lifestyle, with balanced macros designed to burn fat while maintaining muscle.

Do I need to count calories or macros?

We use a simplified point system that makes tracking easy without the stress of counting every calorie. Our system accounts for food choices, planning, sleep, and meditation—helping you stay accountable while keeping things manageable for your busy life.

Can the program accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We work with vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other dietary restrictions. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss your needs and preferences to create a plan that works for you.

What are the "3 rules of fat" you teach?

The 3 rules of fat are fundamental principles we teach about how your body processes and stores fat. These science-backed rules help you understand nutrition in a way that makes sustainable fat loss achievable. You'll learn these during the program.

Do I need to buy special foods or supplements?

No special foods are required! We work with regular grocery store foods. While we do offer Massi protein powder (designed specifically for women over 40), supplements are optional and we'll guide you on what might be beneficial for your specific needs.

How flexible is the meal plan?

Our meal plans are designed to be flexible and adaptable. We understand that life happens—social events, travel, busy schedules. We'll teach you how to make smart choices in any situation while staying on track with your goals.

Training & Exercise

Questions about workouts, training schedules, and exercise programs

How many days per week do I need to exercise?

Our programs typically include 3-5 workout sessions per week, depending on your goals and schedule. We'll create a plan that fits your life—no endless workouts required. Quality and consistency matter more than quantity.

What type of exercises are included?

Our programs focus on strength training, metabolic conditioning, and movement patterns designed for women over 40. Exercises are low-impact, joint-friendly, and can be modified for any fitness level. We emphasize building strength and improving mobility.

What if I have injuries or physical limitations?

We work with many clients who have injuries or limitations. During your assessment, we'll discuss your specific situation and create modifications. Always consult with your healthcare provider, and we'll design a program that respects your body's needs.

How long are the workout sessions?

Workout sessions typically range from 30-45 minutes. We believe in efficient, effective workouts that fit into your busy schedule. You don't need to spend hours in the gym to see results.

Is the training suitable for women over 40?

Yes! All our programs are specifically designed for women over 40, taking into account hormonal changes, metabolism shifts, and the unique needs of midlife bodies. We focus on building strength, preserving muscle mass, and optimizing metabolic health.

Do you provide exercise videos or demonstrations?

Yes! You'll have access to video demonstrations of all exercises, proper form cues, and modifications. Our online platform includes a complete exercise library to guide you through each movement safely and effectively.

Questions about program costs, payment options, and refunds

How much does the program cost?

Do you offer payment plans?

Is there a money-back guarantee?

General Questions

General information about The Fitness Professor and our services

Who is Doug Dorsey, The Fitness Professor?

Doug Dorsey is a best-selling author, certified personal trainer, and transformation coach with over 20 years of experience in the wellness industry and 25 years in education. He specializes in helping women over 40 achieve lasting transformation through science-backed methods.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary by individual, but most clients start noticing changes in energy, strength, and how their clothes fit within the first 4-6 weeks. Sustainable weight loss typically occurs at a rate of 1-2 pounds per week when following the program consistently.

What makes your approach different from other programs?

Our approach is holistic, science-backed, and specifically designed for women over 40. We focus on sustainable habits, not quick fixes. We address the whole person—nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress, and mindset—and provide personalized support every step of the way.

Do you offer online or in-person programs?

We offer both! Our online programs provide flexibility and can be done from anywhere. We also offer in-person training and consultations for those who prefer face-to-face interaction. Many clients combine both approaches.

What support do I receive during the program?

You'll receive weekly live coaching sessions, access to our online community, progress tracking tools, email support, and regular check-ins. We're here to keep you motivated, accountable, and supported throughout your journey.

Can I contact you if I have questions during the program?

Absolutely! We encourage questions and communication. You can reach us via email, through our online platform, or during your scheduled coaching sessions. We're committed to your success and are here to help whenever you need support.

What happens after I complete the program?

After completing the program, you'll have the tools, knowledge, and habits to maintain your results independently. We also offer ongoing support options, maintenance programs, and advanced coaching for those who want continued guidance.
